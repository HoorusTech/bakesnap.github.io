Privacy Policy

BakeSnap - App for Bakers
Last updated: June 24, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how BakeSnap collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We collect: (1) App usage information (analytics); (2) Language preferences and settings; (3) Technical device data; (4) Advertising information (Google Ads). We do not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Information

We use information to: (1) Improve app functionality; (2) Personalize your experience; (3) Display relevant ads; (4) Analyze usage patterns; (5) Fix bugs and technical issues.

Camera and Photos

BakeSnap accesses your camera only when you choose to take photos. Photos are processed locally on your device and are not sent to our servers. You have full control over your images.

Image Analysis

We use Google ML Kit to analyze your photos locally on the device. This analysis includes object detection, quality assessment, and improvement suggestions. Analysis data does not leave your device.

Advertising

We display ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect advertising data according to their own policies. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as described in this policy or when required by law. Anonymous usage data may be shared with Google Analytics.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to: (1) Access your information; (2) Correct incorrect data; (3) Delete your information; (4) Opt out of communications. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be notified through the app. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: contato@hoorustech.com
